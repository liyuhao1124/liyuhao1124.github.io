Yuhao Li (李毓浩)

I am a fourth-year (2021-present) PhD student in the theory group at Columbia University, honored to be advised by Prof. Xi Chen and Prof. Rocco Servedio. Starting from my PhD, I am also extremely fortunate to closely collaborate with Prof. Toniann Pitassi and Prof. Mihalis Yannakakis.

Prior to that, I got the B.Sc. degree in computer science from Peking University, where I was extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiaotie Deng on algorithmic game theory.

I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science and discrete mathematics, including complexity theory (TFNP, proof complexity, communication complexity), game theory, logic and automata theory, and combinatorics.

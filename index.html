Yuhao Li (李毓浩)

I am a first-year (2021-present) PhD student in the theory group of Computer Science Department, Columbia University. I am very fortunately advised by Prof. Xi Chen and Prof. Rocco Servedio.

Before this, I got the B.S. degree in computer science from Peking University in 2021, where I worked with Prof. Xiaotie Deng on algorithmic game theory.

My research interests broadly lie in theoretical computer science.
